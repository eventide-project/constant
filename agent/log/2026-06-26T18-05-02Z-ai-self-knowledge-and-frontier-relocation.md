# Can the AI know its conditioning level and predict the human? Self-knowledge: empirically yes (the ratify/correct record), introspectively no (the subtle-knowledge barrier + the fluency trap — confidence is anti-correlated with hinge-awareness; trust the record not the feeling; the record lags one audit behind). Predicting the human: learnable for expressed taste on seen patterns, not for novel origination — the gap is permanent because prediction relocates the frontier (once the AI absorbs the standard, the human's role is to exceed the now-AI-inclusive mean). Kicker: over-confident prediction freezes the standard, so the provoke-origination channel must persist forever as the engine that keeps the standard rising. Migration reframed: human role migrates to a moving frontier, it does not shrink to zero
