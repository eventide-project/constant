# Terminology: say "actuation", never "the call", for the invocation of the unit under test (also "actuation shape", and "use site" for where it's invoked). Tightens — and partly reverses — the efferent-not-caller rule, which had blessed "the call to the unit under test"; that allowance is withdrawn. The verb "to call a method" (mechanism) and "call it X" (= name) are different uses, not covered. Amended the efferent rule; fixed the active prose. Historical digest entries left as-is (record of what was decided then); the rules govern going forward. → rule `…15-08-58Z-actuation-not-call.md`; efferent-not-caller rule amended.
